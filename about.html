Why LinkedIn?

The most powerful room in business is on LinkedIn.

LinkedIn has over 1 billion members. More than 90% of executives say they consider a candidate or partner's LinkedIn presence before making decisions. It's the single most important professional real estate you own — and most athletes have left it completely vacant.

Unlike Instagram or Twitter, LinkedIn's audience is actively looking for people to do business with. Every post you make is seen by investors, sponsors, brand managers, and CEOs. It's not about followers — it's about the right 10 people seeing the right 10 words at the right time.
